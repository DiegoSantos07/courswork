Climate change

As per the United Nations, human activities such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation have led to a rise in temperatures. This is primarily caused by the release of carbon dioxide into the atmosphere. The gases in the atmosphere act like a shield, trapping the heat on Earth.



Renewable energy

The mitigation of climate change has been a pressing issue for the global community, and the utilisation of renewable energies has been identified as a viable solution. The ubiquity of these alternative sources, ranging from the kinetic energy of flowing rivers to the thermal energy from the sun, has made them increasingly attractive to investors and governments alike. Supporting this trend, the UN has reported that renewable energies are not only environmentally sustainable but also economically advantageous, thus promoting their widespread adoption as a practical strategy for combatting climate change.



Sustainable tips

Choosing organic or plant-based foods is a healthy option that also benefits the environment. Organic farming practices promote soil health and biodiversity while reducing pollution and energy use. Plant-based diets have a lower carbon footprint and require fewer resources than animal-based diets. By making this choice, individuals can positively impact their health as well as the planet's.



To reduce emissions and promote a sustainable future, it's important to choose eco-friendly modes of transportation. Cycling, walking, and public transport are great options for short distances, while electric vehicles are a good alternative to gas-powered cars. By choosing these options, we can reduce our carbon footprint and move towards a greener future.



To reduce plastic pollution, choosing reusable materials over plastic is an effective measure. For example, keeping a used plastic bag from a local supermarket can prevent its disposal and reduce plastic waste. These actions help mitigate plastic usage, minimise ecological footprint, and promote sustainable living while conserving natural resources.
