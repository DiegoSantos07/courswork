Our Mission


Climate Group is committed to the UN's Global Goals and we understand that it is essential for actions to be taken urgently due to the recent climate disasters. We dedicate funds from our recyclable shop to institution which are acting for a green future, we also offer the possibility for volunteering to fight against waste, encourage the use of renewable energy, give financial support and participation on climate action conferences.
